What I have done

At the current moment I have 5 side projects done for my classes including this one. My first minigame is a simple roll a ball concept where the player must gather some objects in order to win and tries to beat their best time on the screen. For my second minigame it is a paint on the canvas where the user can paint using 4 different objects and adjust where there is time to destroy and the color of the objects. For the final minigame it is a FPS monster spawner where the player must kill a certain amount of monsters before being transported to the boss and defeat it to win the game. For the IT114 project it is a simple chatroom with simple features implemented such as chat formatting and commands for games. At the current moment the repository is private in order to prevent plagiarism but I will be able to make it public after the semester.
