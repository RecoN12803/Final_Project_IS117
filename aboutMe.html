About who I am

I am a 3rd year IT major who is specializing in Web Development and a part of the NJIT esports Eboard as well as a tournament organizer and twitch streamer for Highland Masters, a super smash brothers local. I have a huge interest in keyboard that I have had people reach out and ask me to build them mechanical keyboards and I even have a page to submit a commission if you would like. As you have seen on the home page I have interest in vtubers as well, I started watching these streamers around 2018 when it first started popping up on Youtube's recommend page and the algorithm boosting Hololive who is now one of, if not the biggest Vtuber Company. I have watched anime since 2014 and been reading manga since 2017. With all these series under my belt I can say Code Geass is my favorite anime even though there are some caveats with the plot it's one of the most enjoyable animes I have watched. Manga wise, I would have to say that Holyland is one of the greatest stories I have read; it is about a boy who was bullied but slowly gained to the courage to fight back going through many physical and mental trials before emerging as a mature man who protects to the Holyland where he resides.
